Mango Macarons

Mango Macarons-

A light and crispy shell with a soft and chewy middle. The sweet mango jam filling is made with fresh mangos that were cooked down. There is a hint of fresh lemon juice that was added to bring out the mango flavor. This delightful treat is refreshing and light. It is a great option for those who cannot have dairy. It's best to be eaten on the day of delivery, but will be just as great stored in the fridge the next few days.

Pineapple Bread

Pineapple Bread-

A sweet bread with a unique design on it. Pineapple bread
is a type of sweet bread that is usually found in Asian stores.
Despite the name, there is atually not pineapple in it. It is
named that way because of the markings on top. This bread
has a crunchy, sweet top and a pillowy, soft inside. It is baked
fresh on the day of delivery. It is best eaten the day of delivery,
but can be stored overnight in an air-tight container. Microwave
the next day and enjoy the warm and soft bread that tasts like
it just came out of the oven.
