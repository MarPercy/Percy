Earl Gray Macarons

This cookie has a slightly crunchy exterior and a soft interior. It has a strong scent of earl gray and is a great option for those who enjoy tea. It can be enjoyed with a warm cup of earl gray tea. Let it sit out at room temperature for 10-15 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

~blanched almond flour
~powdered sugar
~free range egg whites
~organic unbleached cane sugar
~cream of tartar
~organic fresh cream
~lemon
~earl gray
